About The Dog Bar

The Dog Bar is Florida's premier dog park and bar, offering a unique space where dogs can play freely while their humans enjoy craft cocktails, local beers, and delicious food. Our outdoor dog park is designed for safety and fun, with separate areas for large and small dogs.
